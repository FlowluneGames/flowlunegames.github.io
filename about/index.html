About Flowlune Games

Flowlune Games is an independent UK-based studio focused on building original rhythm game experiences and internally developed gameplay systems.

The studio prioritises clarity, flow, and expressive design. Combining strong technical foundations with a calm, cohesive visual identity.

Founder

Flowlune Games was founded by Joshua Phillips, Founder & Technical Director. Based in the United Kingdom, he focuses on building scalable game architecture and original character-driven IP.

Rhythm Angel is the studio's first public project.
