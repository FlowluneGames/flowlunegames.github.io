Rhythm Angel

Rhythm Angel is a 6-Key keyboard rhythm game built on a custom C++/SDL2 engine, focused on flow, clarity, and musical expression.

The demo is now publicly available. If you play it, I would love to hear what you think. Feedback helps shape difficulty tuning, UI polish, and performance improvements.

Download on itch.io Play on GameJolt

For feedback, issues, or playtesting notes, you can get in touch.

Current status

Status: Demo (In Development)
Platforms: Windows and Linux
Focus: playtesting, difficulty tuning, performance polish, and UI refinements.

If you run into any bugs, please include your OS, specs, and what you were doing when it happened.
