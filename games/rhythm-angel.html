Rhythm Angel

Rhythm Angel is a 6-Key keyboard rhythm game built on a custom C++/SDL2 engine, focused on flow, clarity, and musical expression.

The project is currently in active development, with the demo phase focused on playtesting, performance polish, and feedback.
